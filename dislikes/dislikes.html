Dislikes

Bad A Capella

God gave us talents to share with others, lifting up those around us, sharing his love through them. Nevertheless, “it is not requisite that a man should run faster than he has strength” (Mos. 4:27).

Enthusiasm alone can only take you so far. Pushing bad technique continuously will destroy your vocal chords and hurt our ears along the way. Take your time and don't try too hard to replicate famous singers. Improve instead.

Beans

Beans are my least favorite food. The mushy texture and dirt flavored combination makes me sick to my stomach.

I discovered beans don't like me either, so I'm glad we have at least one thing in common. Put them on the side or don’t include them at all if we’re eating together.
